#1 Welcome to
Digital Business Building


This training endevours to provide you with the knowledge and skills to be a proficient business operator who is able to effectively plan, problem-solve and manage your own business.
Whether you opperate alone, or support a small team... you will learn to build a plan, manage opperations, deal with unpredictable circumstances and measure success with Key Performance Indicators.


#2 Your Learning Experience


This Digital Business Building course has been developed in consultation with industry experts such as
Google, Salesforce and Facebook
as well as professional education and training providers.

It features highly engaging content that is suited to different types of learners.

You will have access to a wide range of material including videos, factsheets, activities, case studies and business planning tools.


#3 Assessment


Each unit can be completed independently, however, they are all closely linked and together form the qualification. The assessment tasks require you to answer questions about case studies and develop your business as you progress through the course.
We encourage you to look for answers beyond the scope of the learning material, either from your own experiences, your workplace or from information you find on the internet.
This will make for a more engaging and richer learning experience.
Successful completion of the assessment tasks will demonstrate your ability to develop your own business and apply the theory you have learnt to practical situations.

#4 The first step is identifying your starting point...


START-UP ☞ identify problems to solve and develop them into opportunities;
IMPROVE ☞ review through data & feedback your existing products and services; or
SCALE ☞ build more value back to your existing customers.

The great thing is... it doesn't matter where you are, understanding and implementing all of the above is the key to establishing a successful brand and business.
