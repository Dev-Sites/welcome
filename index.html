Welcome to  Building a Digital Business

This training endeavours to provide you with the knowledge and skills to be a proficient business operator who is able to effectively plan, problem-solve and manage your own business.

Whether you operate alone, or support a small team... you will learn to build a plan, manage operations, deal with unpredictable circumstances and measure success with Key Performance Indicators (KPI's).

Your Learning Experience


This Digital Business Building course has been developed in consultation with industry experts such as Google, Salesforce and Facebook as well as professional education and training providers. 

It features highly engaging content that is suited to different types of learners. 

You will have access to a wide range of material including videos, factsheets, activities, case studies and business planning tools.

Assessment

Each unit can be completed independently, however, they are all closely linked and together form the qualification. The assessment tasks require you to answer questions about case studies and develop your business as you progress through the course.
We encourage you to look for answers beyond the scope of the learning material, either from your own experiences, your workplace or from information you find on the internet.
This will make for a more engaging and richer learning experience.
Successful completion of the assessment tasks will demonstrate your ability to develop your own business and apply the theory you have learnt to practical situations.

Identifying your starting point

START-UP ☞ Identify problems to solve and develop them into opportunities;

IMPROVE ☞ review data & feedback of existing products and services; or

SCALE ☞ build more value back to your existing customers.

It doesn't matter where you are, understanding and implementing all of the above is the key to establishing a successful brand and business.

What you will learn.

Successful Digital Business Building is a process over time.

Build a Business Map

1. Develop your unique niche market and product & service suite offering;
2. Complete market research and competitive analysis;
3. Build your ideal customer avatar;
4. Articulate your brand strengths, core values, and purpose;
5. Build your brand promise & ultimate brand pitch; and
6. Create your financial budget & identify your key metrics for success.

Omni-Channel Digital Assets

1. Utilise Canva for your digital images;
2. Create video assets & design visual components for web and social media;
3. Use social media tools & marketing for collaboration and engagement;
4. Understand blogging & create content; &
5. Explore online advertising, affiliate and email marketing.

Website Development & Maintenance

1. Build your business website with eCommerce facilities;
2. Update, review & maintain your website;
3. Install, optimise operating system software & run standard diagnostic tests;
4. Create your own web-to-lead strategy using a landing page; &
5. Create links & add interactivity.

At the completion of your training... you will have built a complete digital platform for your business.
